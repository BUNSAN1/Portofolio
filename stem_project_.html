Programming STEM Project - [Converting Prefix Unit in Length]

Describe your project. What is the topic? What is the problem? What is the input? What is the output?
This programming will convert the prefix unit of Length. You can convert cm to m, mm to nm, or anything you want.
The inputs are number , its unit(Mm, km, m, dm, cm, mm, μm, nm, or pm), and the unit that you want to convert. The output is converted length.
It takes three steps to convert. First, convert given prefix unit to m. Next, convert m to unit we want.
